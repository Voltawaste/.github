## ⚡ About Voltawaste

> *"Powering smarter, greener e-waste management — one device at a time."*

**Voltawaste** is an IoT-based e-waste management platform focused on **smart collection**, **real-time tracking**, and **sustainable recycling**. We build technology that bridges the gap between consumers, collectors, and recycling facilities.

---

## 🌱 Our Mission

To **reduce environmental impact** by reimagining how electronic waste is managed — using intelligent IoT systems, real-time data, and accessible digital tools to make e-waste disposal smarter and more sustainable for everyone.
